Decade Highlight

The 1970s introduced the first home consoles, using cartridges and very simple graphics.

  • First home systems
  • Black & white graphics
  • Game cartridges begin

Notable 1970s Consoles

  • Magnavox Odyssey (1972)
  • Atari 2600 (1977)
  • Fairchild Channel F (1976)

The Magnavox Odyssey was the first home video game console, while the Atari 2600 popularized interchangeable game cartridges and home arcade experiences.

Although graphics were basic, these systems created the foundation for the entire video game industry.
